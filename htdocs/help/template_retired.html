Archived Templates - Advanced/Simple

Use Archived Templates, to search Krang for archived Templates. By using search terms and keywords, you can run specific searches for individual or multiple templates.

There are two search modes:

  • Simple: The default search is very simple: just enter a title, URL or keyword and click Search. For a complete list of Templates, leave the space blank and click Search.
  • Advanced: This search allows you to be more specific about the Template you're trying to find. Fill in any or all of the fields and click Search. To restart your search with a fresh page, click Clear.
    When searching by Template ID, you may also find live or trashed templates. For live templates the status column will indicate 'Live', for trashed templates, 'Trash'.

After completing your Search and finding the Template you are looking for, you can View, Unarchive or Delete the Template (depending on your permission set).

If you didn't find what you are looking for, you may click Search in Live to start the same search in the live Krang.

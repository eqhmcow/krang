Edit Template

If you need to make any changes to your Template, you will make these modifications on the Edit Template screen.

The Edit Template Screen has the following editable fields:

  • Upload File: Click on Browse to find a file on your computer. This is not required.
  • Template code: Create/edit your template directly in the CMS. Use this text box for code.
  • Test with this template: Check this box and you can test the template without deploying it and affecting stories being published.

Actions

At the bottom of the Edit Template page, there are buttons and different actions to choose from.

  • Revert: You can revert to a previously saved version. Select the version number from the drop-down and click on Revert. If you prefer, you can also View a previously saved version.
  • Save: Click on Save to save your Template. Clicking on this button will take you up a level to 'My Workspace'.
  • Save & Stay: Click on Save & Stay to save your Template and remain on the Edit Template screen.
  • Deploy: Click on Deploy and your template will be deployed. Templates that are deployed will be used to format Stories that are published.
  • Reset: Clicking on Reset will reset all text fields to last Saved version.
  • Delete: Click on Delete to delete your Template from the CMS.

In addition to the Action buttons featured at the bottom of the page, the following is available at the top:

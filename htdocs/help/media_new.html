New Media

The New Media page is used to upload or create media, such as images (.jpg, .gif) and video, for use in your stories. Any media that you want to display with your story has to be uploaded to Krang. Fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required fields and must be entered.

  • Type: Each Media has a Type. This is set by the CMS on creation of a New Media and is not an editable field. If you selected the incorrect Media Type, you will need to return to 'New Media.' Media Type selection determines the attributes available to the Media.
  • Title: Required field used to give your image a unique name.
  • Site/Categories: Required field used to designate the URL of the site or category where you want to upload your media. Clicking the Choose button will launch a menu tree where you can find the correct category. From the menu tree click OK to accept your choice and close the function or Cancel to return to the New Media page.
  • Upload File: Click on Browse to find an image or other media you would like to upload. This is a required field for binary types; otherwise you may leave it blank, and Krang will attempt to create an empty file named after your Title.
  • Default Caption: Text field where you can enter a default caption to associate with your media. This is an optional field and may not appear within the output depending on the template configuration.
  • Default Copyright: Text field where you can enter a default copyright to associate with your media. This is an optional field and may not appear within the output depending on the template configuration.
  • Alt Tag: Text field where you can enter an alt tag to associate with your media. Alt tags are displayed on a web page when the image is not available or spoken by a screen reader.
  • Notes: Optional field where you can enter information about the image. Notes are not displayed in a story.
  • Contributors: A contributor can be any person, ex. writer or photographer who contributed to the Media. Click on Edit Contributors to associate, or un-associate your contributors. See Associate Contributors for additional Help.

After entering in the above information click on one of the buttons at the bottom of page. Clicking on Save will save your media and return you to your Workspace. Save & Stay will save your media and refresh the current page.

  • Reset is also available. Resetting will delete all text from the text boxes on this page since the last Save.

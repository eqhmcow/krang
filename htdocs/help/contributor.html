Contributors

A contributor is a person who contributes content to your web site and therefore important to attribute Stories or photos to specific people. By adding Contributors within the Contributor Manager and later attaching them to content items, you can recognize writers, photographers, etc. easily throughout your web site.

The following functions are available within the Contributor Manager:

Search:Search for a Contributor by First Name or Last Name. Do a blank search to list all Contributors.
Add:Click on Add A Contributor, enter in the requested information and click Save to save your Contributor.
Edit: Click on Edit to modify a Contributor profile.
Delete: To delete a contributor, check the check box and click on Delete Checked.

Find Media - Advanced/Simple

Use Find Media to search Krang for live media. By using search terms and keywords, you can run specific searches for individual or multiple media.

There are two Find Media interfaces.

  • Simple: The default search is very simple: just enter a title, URL or keyword and click Search. For a complete list of Media, leave the space blank and click Search.
  • Advanced: This search allows you to be more specific about the Media you're trying to find. Fill in any or all of the fields and click Search. To restart your search with a fresh page, click Clear.
    When searching by Media ID, you may also find archived or trashed media. For archived media the status column will indicate 'Archive', for trashed media, 'Trash'.

After completing your search and finding the Media you are looking for, you can View, Edit, Archive, Check Out, Publish or Delete the Media (depending on your permission set).

If you didn't find what you are looking for, you may click Search in Archive to start the same search in the Archive.

Find Story - Advanced/Simple

Use Find Story, to search Krang for live stories. By using search terms and keywords, you can run specific searches for individual or multiple stories.

There are two Find Story interfaces.

  • Simple: The default search is very simple: just enter a title, URL or keyword and click Search. (To include the actual content of Stories in your search, click 'Search full text?') For a complete list of Stories, leave the space blank and click Search.
  • Advanced: This search allows you to be more specific about the Story you're trying to find. Fill in any or all of the fields and click Search. To restart your search with a fresh page, click Clear.
    When searching by Story ID, you may also find archived or trashed stories. For archived stories the status column will indicate 'Archive', for trashed stories, 'Trash'.

After completing your Search and finding the Story you are looking for, you can Edit, Check Out, View, Copy, Archive, Publish or Delete the Story (depending on your permission set).

If you didn't find what you are looking for, you may click Search in Archive to start the same search in the Archive.

Note Regarding Full-Text Searches
Phrases can be put in quotation marks if you wish to find Stories that contain the whole phrase rather than the individual words. Also, although the default search matches partial words, you can request full-word matches only by quoting the text and including whitespace - for example, " word " will not match "wordsmith", but will match "word" even if it appears at the beginning or end of a sentence.

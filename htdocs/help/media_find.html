Find Media - Advanced/Simple

Use Find Media, to search the PIRT CMS for media. By using search terms and keywords, you can run specific searches for individual or multiple media.

There are two types of Find Media.

1) Simple
2) Advanced

Simple: The default search is very simple: just enter a title, URL or keyword and click Search. For a complete list of Media, leave the space blank and click Search.

Advanced: This search allows you to be more specific about the Media you're trying to find. Fill in any or all of the fields and click Search. To restart your search with a fresh page, click Clear.

After completing your search and finding the Media you are looking for, you can edit, check out, view, or delete the Media.

Schedule Editor

At the bottom of the Edit Story/Media page, clicking on Publish will publish your Story immediately. However, you can also schedule your Story/Media to publish at a specific time or day of the week. Click on Schedule at the top of the page to go to the Schedule Editor.

Simple

The Schedule Editor defaults to a simple view. Within the simple view you can schedule your file to be published at a future day or time. From the dropdown menus select month, day, year, hour, minute, am or pm. Click on Add to Schedule. Your story will be published at this future date.

You can also specify a version for publish. The publish scheduler defaults to the newest version, but if you want a different version to publish at a certain time, you can select a different version from the dropdown menu.

To schedule more advanced functions, click on Advanced Schedule.

Advanced Schedule

Advanced schedules allows you to publish a Story or Media at a pre-determined time monthly, weekly, daily or hourly. Select the radio button for the time you choose, then select from the dropdowns, the day, hour, or month.

After selecting the timeframe, you need then to select the version, either the newest version or a previous one. From the advanced schedule page, you a can schedule your Stories/Media to expire. Essentially, to expire means to delete from the file system and remove from the Internet. Expired Stories will still appear in the CMS. To expire a Story, follow the same process for Publish, except choose Expire from the action drop down menu.

Click Return to return to the Edit Story/Media page.

New Story

Within the PIRT CMS, all written content is considered a Story. Whether it's a feature article or your web site's Homepage, using this tool will get you started creating new content.

Each Story that is created in the PIRT CMS will have unique elements and specific features that you pick and choose from as you build your Story.

The first items associated with a Story are the Properties that you select/enter on the 'New Story' page.

Type: Type refers to your Story Type. Select the type of Story you'd like to create, such as Feature Article. Story Type is a required field.
Title: This is the Title of your Story. Depending on the Story Type it may appear directly in the output. The Title is a required field.
Slug: A one-word identifier used as the last part of the Story's URI, and the directory in which the story resides. Some Story Types require a slug. Essentially, this is the story's filename and will appear in the URL of the page. The slug must be unique.
Cover Date: When creating a New Story, select a Cover Date. The cover Date defaults to the current date and time, but it can be modified to a future or previous date. The cover date is a required field and may appear on some Story Types directly in the URL or the page.
Site/Category: Every Story must be located in a site and category. Select the appropriate file path by clicking Choose. Then select a site and category from the popup window. A site/category is a required field.

After the above information is entered, click on Create, this will take you to the next step in Creating a New Story.

Reset and Cancel are also available. Resetting will delete all text from the text boxes on this page. Canceling will return you to My Workspace and not Save any of the information entered.

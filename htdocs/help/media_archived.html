Archived Media - Advanced/Simple

Use Archived Media, to search Krang for archived media. By using search terms and keywords, you can run specific searches for individual or multiple media.

There are two search modes:

  • Simple: The default search is very simple: just enter a title, URL or keyword and click Search. For a complete list of Media, leave the space blank and click Search.
  • Advanced: This search allows you to be more specific about the Media you're trying to find. Fill in any or all of the fields and click Search. To restart your search with a fresh page, click Clear.
    When searching by Media ID, you may also find live or trashed media. For live media the status column will indicate 'Live', for trashed media, 'Trash'.

After completing your search and finding the Media you are looking for, you can View, Unarchive or Delete the Media (depending on your permission set).

If you didn't find what you are looking for, you may click Search in Live to start the same search in live Krang.

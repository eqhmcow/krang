Schedule Editor

At the bottom of the Edit Story page, clicking on Publish will publish your Story immediately. However, you can also schedule your Story to publish at a specific time or day. Click on Schedule at the top of the page to go to the Story Schedule Editor.

Simple

The Story Schedule Editor defaults to a Simple view. Within the Simple View you can schedule your Story to be published at a later day. From the dropdown menus select Month, Day, Year, Hour, Minute, AM or PM. Click on Add to Schedule. Your story will be published at this future date.

You can also specify a Version for publish. The publish scheduler defaults to the Newest Version, but if you want a different version to publish at a certain time, you can select a different version from the dropdown menu.

To schedule more advanced functions, click on Advanced Schedule.

Advanced Schedule

Advanced schedules allows you to publish a Story at a pre-determined time monthly, weekly, daily or hourly. Select the radio button for the time you choose, then select from the dropdowns, the day hour or month.

Then select the Version, either the Newest version or a previous one. From the Advanced Schedule page, you a can also Expire your Stories. Essentially, to expire, means to delete from the file system and remove from the Internet. Expired Stories will still appear in the CMS. To expire a Story, follow the same process for Publish, except choose Expire from the Action drop down menu.

Click Return to return to the Edit Story page.
